Christion McLeran's Guide to Mastering Photoshop

An Instruction Challenge for JOUR 108

The 5 steps to learning Photoshop on your own

Time Needed: 1-6 Hours
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

Are you a first semester journalism student who needs to learn the basics of photoshop but doesn't like asking for help? No need to worry, with these 5 simple steps you'll be on your way to mastering the intimidating program!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Access to a computer with Photoshop
  • A Miracle
  • Anger manegment skills
  • A break after about 15 minutes

  1. Try your best to just wing it.

    You used KidPix on the computers in middle school, how much different could Photoshop really be?.

  2. Realize you're in way over your head and that this is nothing like KidPix from middle shool.

    This is an important step in realizing how cocky you can be, there are 70 tools at your disposal and you dont know what to do with any of them.

    >

  3. Question your current major.

    You know what, maybe your folks were right and you should have pursued forestry. I mean, park rangers probably dont have to use photoshop, right?

  4. Accept your fate.

    This can be done before or after crying on the floor in the fetal position, but that photoshop assignment isn't going to do itself.

  5. Realize that Youtube has tons of tutorials to help with your assignment.

    Honestly, what would we do without Youtube?.
